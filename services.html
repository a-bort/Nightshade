You want plants? We've got plants!

If you have an urban outdoor space that needs some love--be it a yard, balcony, rooftop, or that overgrown thicket of weeds you’ve seen the rats hanging out in--we want to help you transform it into something more valuable and enriching.

Culinary landscape design

We work with you to design and create a customized landscape that will take your cooking and drink-making to the next level

Planting events

We provide custom herb/veggie/fruit installations and hands-on training

Consultations

We offer expert advice on gardening, herbs, cooking, and cocktails

Our projects work alongside nature, creating a more enriching and sustainable urban environment. In addition to becoming a plant-growing badass, you’ll get the benefits of an attractive and practical outdoor space that enhances your culinary prowess. We’ll work with you to customize a culinary landscape--one that works for your tastebuds, your budget, and your specific outdoor space. We’ll also make life easy for you by:

  • Helping select and install your choice of herbs, veggies, or fruit that works for your palate, your culinary goals, and your specific growing space
  • Providing 24/7 access to our custom plant care app that will empower you to keep your plants thriving and your meals and drinks fresher than ever
  • Keeping your menu fresh and innovative by coming out to you every Spring, Summer, and Fall to plant seasonal produce and provide specific plant care tutorials
  • Performing any seasonal garden maintenance for you
