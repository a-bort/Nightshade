Our Values

We strive to uphold our values...in our personal lives and in our business. These are the values our business is built around. For a deeper dive, check out our manifesto.

Do you want to smell ridiculously amazing smells single every day of your life? Us too. Fresh, homegrown herbs and produce help bring out the full potential in the dishes and drinks you work so hard to create. Enjoy impossibly fresh ingredients that blow the grocery store’s packaged crap out of the water.

We give our plants multi-vitamins. Sort of. Did you know that adding trace minerals into the soil provides plants with all of the micro-nutrients they need to create a plethora of vitamins, minerals, and best of all, those glorious aromatic flavor compounds. Supermarket produce doesn’t come close in terms of nutrient density, even the fancy “organic” stuff.

Fact: Nature is utterly gorgeous. It also provides an amazing getaway from the stress and anxiety that can occasionally get the best of us all. Time and time again, science has shown the multitude of benefits of spending time in nature!

Buzzkill: The leading cause of biodiversity loss is habitat destruction and degradation. In addition to providing produce to you, our landscapes aim to create habitat and forage for birds, pollinators, and other beneficial critters. Your plants will provide a safe haven for life to flourish. You’ll also be mitigating soil loss, reducing runoff, and lowering your fossil fuel footprint.

We believe that the ability to provide you and your loved ones with an essential human need is a preeeetty important life skill. We’ll get back to you when we figure out how to produce air and water (right after giving our Nobel Prize acceptance speech). Let us give you a kick start on your way to more liberty and less reliance on corporations and industrialized agriculture. We encourage everyone to become a more self-sufficient member of society and this planet!
